Blackened twisted and fractured marrow trees sprout all upon the surface of Tir Latha, infecting the forest now referred to as "The Gloaming".

Deformed fleshy beings emerge from the husks of the branches and slink to the ground ready to feast.

Their arrival has turned the skies a sickly pale red during the day, and a starless void at night.

A member of the [[Lore/Factions/Knights of St. Ydris\|Knights of St. Ydris]].
Spends most of his time in the Greywall Priory's infirmary caring for knights who have taken on injuries (and survived).

He is the oldest member, and has an interest in painting.

Garian is related to [[Lore/NPCs/Harus\|Harus]] (Brother).
